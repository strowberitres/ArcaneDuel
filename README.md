Arcane Duel is a 2-player browser game inspired by the magical world of Harry Potter. Players are randomly sorted into Houses and face off in a duel using a variety of spells, each with unique animations, cooldowns, and sound effects.

Dynamic Spell System:

Skill Cooldowns (CD): Strategic gameplay requiring players to manage spell availability.
Visual Effects: unique particle/visual effects for beams, lightning, and shields.
Audio Cues: Immersive sound effects triggered by every skill usage.
Stickman Animations: Characters perform distinct actions and poses corresponding to the specific spell cast.
Random House Sorting: Upon loading, players are randomly assigned a Hogwarts House (e.g., Gryffindor, Ravenclaw).
Health & Resource Management: Full HP bars and status tracking.
